# Elevator Pitch

**Target length:** ~45–55 seconds spoken (~115 words)

---

## Script

Most LMS platforms are either outdated admin tools or AI demos that never ship.

**IntelliGen LMS** is a full-stack, AI-powered learning platform I built to show what production-ready ed-tech looks like.

Instructors use AI to generate courses and quizzes in minutes. Learners get a modern player, gamification, corporate training games, and a context-aware assistant. On completion, they receive verifiable certificates. Admins see real analytics — enrollment trends, completion rates, leaderboards — in one dashboard.

It’s **87 features**, enterprise patterns like 2FA and SSO, multi-tenant orgs, and it’s live today on Vercel.

If you’re hiring for full-stack work, evaluating LMS vendors, or building training for a team — this is the demo I’d walk you through in five minutes.

**intelligen-lms.vercel.app**

## Tips for delivery

- Lead with the problem (slow authoring, weak engagement, no proof of ROI), then position IntelliGen as the answer.
- Offer the **product tour** for async viewers and **demo accounts** for live screen shares.
- For technical audiences, mention Next.js, Prisma, and PostgreSQL; for business audiences, emphasize certificates, analytics, and time-to-launch.
